Instructions

This is a demographic survey for a research study being conducted by the Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluations at the University of Washington. We are seeking information about Twitter users for inferring demographics on individuals who tweet about disease and public health to improve the use of this data for public health research. You must be an active Twitter user (with more than 100 tweets) and have had an account for more than a year to be eligible for this survey. To receive credit for completing this survey, a twitter handle is required and accounts will be validated. Please answer all questions. Your answers will be kept confidential and we will not share your personal information with anyone outside of the research study. This study is being sponsored by the Robert Wood Johnson Foundation. You must be 18 years or older to participate.
